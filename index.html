About Us

The Cornell Taiwanese American Society (CTAS) is a non-political, social club open to anyone from Cornell. Our goal is to promote a better understanding of Taiwanese culture in the Cornell community while providing a fun and memorable experience for our members. We are proud to be a diverse club composed of individuals of many different ages, majors, and ethnicities so come check us out!

Meetings

Our general body events are typically held every Friday at 5pm in Willard Straight Hall Music Room.

ITASA

We are a proud member of the Intercollegiate Taiwanese American Student Association which helps connect our members to the larger Taiwanese American community.

Discounts

Our members have discounts to local restaurants including (but not limited to) Apollo, Koko, Oishii Bowl, Plum Tree, Calios, and Panda Tea Lounge.

Bubble Tea Social

Free bubble tea and new friends

Our first event of the year! Enjoy free homemade bubble tea and mingle with other Taiwan-loving people!

Night Market

Delicious homemade Taiwanese food

Taiwan is famous for its radiant, fun-filled night markets, and we're letting you experience one right here at Cornell. Try some delicious homemade Taiwanese food and play fun games!

Ice Time!

Ice skate with your CTAS family

Come ice skate at the beautiful Lynah Rink and keep yourself warm with some hot chocolate!

Hot Pot Night

Warm up with some savory broth

Warm up with some family-style hot pot during the cold Ithaca winter and participate in CTAS's annual Secret Santa.

eCsTASy

CTAS's annual culture show

Like humor? Like Taiwan? Come to eCsTASy, our annual cultural show filled with hilarious skits, tear-jerking music videos, and live dance performances, now in collaboration with HKSA!

End of the Year Picnic

Celebrate the end of the year with CTAS

What better way to conclude the school year with picnics at one of the local parks? Play sports, socialize, and stuff yourself with grilled hamburgers, hot dogs, etc. Who knows, your favorite eboard member might even get thrown into the lake!
